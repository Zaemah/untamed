Untamed: A Memoir

by Glennon Doyle

Untamed is a powerful memoir that has left a lasting impact on my life. The memoir shares the story of Glennon Doyle's journey towards self-discovery and empowerment. With raw emotion and unbridled honesty, Glennon recounts her struggles with addiction, motherhood, and marriage, and how she found the courage to break free from societal expectations and embrace her true self. Her story has inspired me to embrace my own vulnerabilities and live a more authentic life.The book is a powerful reminder that we are all wild and untamed, and that we have the power to break free from the societal expectations and constraints that hold us back. It's a call to embrace our true selves and live life on our own terms. I love how Glennon weaves together stories from her own life with universal truths and lessons that are relatable to everyone. Her writing is both entertaining and thought-provoking, making it a truly unforgettable read.

A powerful memoir of Love, Life and Triumph

Interesting Facts about Untamed

  • Untamed was a New York Times bestseller
  • Glennon Doyle wrote the book during a 40-day solo road trip across the country
  • It was during a difficult time in her life, struggling with addiction and her decision to leave her marriage
  • The book has been translated into several languages
  • Glennon's husband, designed the cover art for the book

My Personal Connection to Untamed

I first discovered Untamed during a difficult time in my life, when I was struggling to find my own sense of purpose and identity. Glennon's words resonated deeply with me, and her story inspired me to embrace my own vulnerabilities and imperfections. The book became a source of comfort and strength for me, and I have since read it multiple times, always finding new nuggets of wisdom and insight.
